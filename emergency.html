Emergency Resources (US)

If you’re in immediate danger, call emergency services right away.

Home Emergency

Immediate Danger

  • Call 911 (Emergency Services)

Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

  • Call or text 988
  • Chat available online: search “988 chat”

Crisis Text Line

  • Text HOME to 741741

Domestic Violence Hotline

  • Call 1-800-799-7233
  • Text START to 88788

Sexual Assault (RAINN)

  • Call 1-800-656-HOPE (4673)
  • Online chat: search “RAINN chat”

LGBTQ+ Support (The Trevor Project)

  • Call 1-866-488-7386
  • Text START to 678678

Poison Control

  • Call 1-800-222-1222

Compass is not a substitute for professional care. If you need urgent help, reach out to the resources above.
